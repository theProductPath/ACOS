ACOS
Agentic Company Operating System

The operating system for running your business with AI agents.

Your company already runs on scattered folders, docs, and tools. ACOS turns that sprawl into one coherent context surface any AI agent can read, orient itself in, and act on — without you re-explaining your business every time. LLM-agnostic, tool-agnostic, bring your own folders.

Cascade

Agents read your folder READMEs top-down and always land with the right context.

Templates

Ready-made briefs, manifests, and dashboards — copy in, fill out, done.

Skills

Reusable agent capabilities that run against any ACOS instance out of the box.

Scale

Codify how your company works once; every agent, on every model, inherits it.
